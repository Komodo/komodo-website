EuroSciPy 2015

Aug 25, 2015 to Aug 29, 2015 https://www.euroscipy.org/2015/

The EuroSciPy meeting is a cross-disciplinary gathering focused on the use and development of the Python language in scientific research. This event strives to bring together both users and developers of scientific tools, as well as academic research and state of the art industry. Held in Cambridge, UK this year, sign up to see presentations of scientific tools and libraries using the Python language.

Kiwi PyCon 2015

Sep 3, 2015 to Sep 6, 2015 https://nzpug.org/

Kiwi PyCon is an annual conference aimed at promoting and educating people about the Python programming language.

The New Zealand Python User Group is proud to present the seventh national Python conference in New Zealand.

EuroPython 2015

Jul 19, 2015 to Jul 25, 2015 https://ep2015.europython.eu/en/

Hosted in Bilbao, Spain this year, EuroPython 2015 is the 14th EuroPython conference. The conference tours throughout Europe, and so far has had stops in Belgium, Sweden, Lithuania, United Kingdom, Italy and Germany, growing from initially 240 attendees to over 1200 last year.

Code for Canada Vancouver - June 2015

Jun 3, 2015 to Jun 3, 2015 http://www.meetup.com/Code-for-Canada-Vancouver/events/

Code for Canada was inspired by Code for America's Code for All movement. A group of Canucks from Vancouver to Toronto who believe that digital technology opens new channels for citizens to more meaningfully engage in the public sphere and have a positive impact on their communities in Canada.

The ActiveState Komodo team is the proud beverage sponsor for this month's meetup: http://www.meetup.com/Code-for-Canada-Vancouver/events/

DeveloperWeek 2016

Join us at DeveloperWeek in SFO this February!

ActiveState will be at DevWeek showcasing Komodo IDE 9 as well as talking about our plans for Komodo 10. Proud sponsors, we'll be at booth 235.

PyOhio 2015

Jul 31, 2015 to Aug 1, 2015 http://www.pyohio.org/

ActiveState Komodo is a proud media sponsor of PyOhio! A free annual conference for Python programmers in and around Ohio, the entire Midwest, maybe even the whole world.

DjangoCon US 2015

Sep 5, 2015 to Sep 10, 2015 https://2015.djangocon.us/

Komodo IDE is a proud bronze sponsor of DjangoCon. DjangoCon has something for everyone, from the person who develops Django applications for a living, to the person who just tinkers in their spare time. Whether you're a Django expert, just started working in Django recently, or are interested in learning, we have talks and tutorials for you. DjangoCon is a great place to learn, exchange ideas, and meet great people.
